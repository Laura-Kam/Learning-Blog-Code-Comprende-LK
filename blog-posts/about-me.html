About Me

Hi, I’m Laura — ex-ESL teacher turned freelance front-end developer and founder of CodeComprende.

When I first started learning to code, I constantly heard: “Just Google it.” But let’s be real — blog posts and videos often skip the basics. As adult learners, we’re left piecing things together and hoping it clicks.

That didn’t sit right with me. So, I set out to change it. With my experience in the classroom, I now design scaffolded learning journeys that meet you where you are — without the overwhelm.
